    His Excellency Mr. Arkan Al Shaibani

    His Excellency the Minister of Communications, Mr. Arkan Al-Shaibani, spoke during the forum about the technical reality in Iraq and the obstacles facing the Ministry in developing technology. On the other hand, His Excellency mentioned that more than 35 major projects have been launched during the past two years. As for the Internet, in just a year and a half, its speed has increased by more than 40 places in the world rankings.
