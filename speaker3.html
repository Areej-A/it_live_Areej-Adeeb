    Batool Hussein

    Batool Hussein Al-Saadi The founder of startup ABcode for programming and enrichment education, which teaches programming to children in cooperation with the American organization Code.org® and the basics of architecture and 3D modeling using curricula affiliated with Google. • She won the first place award as the best female entrepreneur in Rai'dat Program 2020, organized by the French Embassy, the Francophone University Agency, and the Station Foundation for Entrepreneurship. • She designed and co-authored a number of enrichment curricula, including – a genius guide with math GGMath - in mathematics and a series of computer principles for the elementary stages in Al-Ameed Education Group. • Held several international events and involved Iraqi students with the world, including Hour Of Code - Scratch Day - Iraq next coders INC'19. • Batool holds Bachelor degree in Computer Science – the University of Karbala She has 6 years of experience in entrepreneurship. Certifications: • Teaching computer science fundamentals from the American organization for programming curricula Code.org® • The Shield of Creativity and Excellence from the International Federation of Creators in Iraq. • Certificate of authorship and design of enrichment curricula from Al-Ameed Educational Group. • Certificate of entrepreneurship courses for Iraqpreneurs and reaching the elite level in Iraqpreneurs 2020 • A recommendation to investors from the German Corporation for International Cooperation (GIZ) and Iraqpreneurs. • Certificate from (YSESD) Project for Entrepreneurship - Rutgers University - New Jersey - USA
